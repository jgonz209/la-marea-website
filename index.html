charmander

From Wallpaper Access

Charmander can be found in hot, mountainous areas. Charmander is one of the most well behaved Pokémons. Its feelings and emotions can be read by the flame on its tail. If Charmander is sick or damaged, the flame will flicker and become little and dull. When it growls, it's either angry or about to attack.

eevee

From WallpaperBat

Eevee has an unevenly formed genetic structure that is easily impacted by its surroundings. This helps it to evolve and adapt to a range of habitats. Eevee has the ability to evolve into eight different forms. Eevee can also change its appearance to that of the Trainer who controls it.

umbreon

From Wallpaper Cave

The patterns on their fur shine and fill up with energy when exposed to moonlight. The energy is released at night or when they are aroused. When angered, they can spew a cloud of noxious gas from its sweat glands to stun their attacker. Umbreon's are nocturnal creatures with excellent night vision.
